Romualdus Salernitanus Chronicon

A scholarly digital edition of the Chronicon by Romualdus Salernitanus (XII Century), edited by Paolo Monella within the ALIM Project (2020):

  • GitHub repository of the edition: https://github.com/paolomonella/romualdus (also including the edition TEI XML source file)
  • HTML visualization of the edition in the ALIM digital library: http://alim.unisi.it/dl/resource/299
    • My Introduction is only readable in the TEI XML source file.
  • [Demo] synoptic two-layers visualization of the first paragraphs only (g3.2-3.6, g3.7-3.16 and g3.17-4.2) of the text in MSS A, B and C.
